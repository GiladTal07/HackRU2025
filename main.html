Description

Our AI-powered system analyzes a user’s resume to identify their major, current skills, and experience. It then suggests a range of potential career paths that align with their background. After the user selects a preferred career path, the AI compares their existing skills to those commonly required in that field and highlights the specific skills they may need to develop — providing a personalized roadmap for career growth.
